We welcome you to the website for the Ministry of Magic on the Final Fantasy XIV Excalibur server.
Our FC was originally founded in roughly Feburary of 2017, and we purchased our first FC house only a little later.
Since then we have lived through the Stormblood expansion, and are going into Shadowbringers with a strong community.
We now have a Mansion in Shirogane, a fully decked fleet of Airships & Submersibles, a very active discord, and over 50 members.
